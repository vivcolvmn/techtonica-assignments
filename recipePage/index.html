Viv's Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

This classic eggplant parmesan recipe is a favorite for Italian cuisine lovers. It's a perfect combination of crispy breaded eggplant, rich marinara sauce, and melted cheese, all garnished with fresh basil>

Photo credit: eatingwell.com

  • 2 medium sized eggplants (about 2 punds total)
  • salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregeno
  • 1/2 a teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 and 1/2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 and 1/4 cups shredded mozerella
  • 2 tablespoons minced basil for garnish
  1. Move oven rack so that it is in the second from the top space. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Brush 2 tablespoons of olive oil on a large baking sheet and set aside.
  2. Cut the eggplants into lengthwise pieces about 1 inch thick. Sprinkle salt on both sides and let sit for 30 min or longer.
  3. After 30 minutes, pat the eggplant dry with a paper towel, then season with salt and pepper
  4. In one shallow dish, whisk eggs
  5. In another shallow dish, mix together panko bread crumbs, grated parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregeno, and black pepper.
  6. Dip one eggplant slice into the egg, allow excess to drip off. Then dip it into the breadcrumb mixture until it is fully coated in breadcrumbs. Press crumbs firmly against eggplant with your hands. Place on prepared baking sheet, repeat with remaining eggplant.
  7. Place baking sheet in the oven on the second from the top rack. Bake for 20 minutes then flip and bake for another 20 min, or until eggplant is tender and breading is golden.
  8. Pour 1/4 cup of marinara sauce over each eggplant, then top with cheese. Place back in the oven and bake 10 more minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Garnish with freshly chopped basil.
